The Method

Most rooms sell signals.
We teach a system.

Anyone can post a green candle. What lasts is a repeatable process you can run on your worst day. These four principles run under every callout, every session, every trade we take.

01

Risk-First Sizing

Position size is decided before the entry, not after. We define what we’re willing to lose first — the upside takes care of itself.

02

Written Invalidation

Every idea ships with a line in the sand. If price proves the thesis wrong, we’re out — no hoping, no averaging into losers.

03

Process Over Hype

No moon-boy promises. We grade ourselves on whether we followed the plan, not whether a single trade printed.

04

Journal & Review

What gets measured gets better. We log entries, exits and emotion, then review as a team so the same mistake doesn’t cost twice.
