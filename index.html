Discord Access Setup

Once you have Basic Access, you may automatically be in the server. But if you don't have a Discord account connected to Patreon or a Discord account in general, this will help.

Discord Registration Discord Download
  1. Go to Settings in Patreon
  2. Click “More” dropdown
  3. Select “Connected Apps”
  4. Click Discord
  5. Press Connect

After connecting, our Discord server should appear automatically. Restart Discord if needed.
