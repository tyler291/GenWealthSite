1Information We Collect

If you contact us, participate in the community, or submit information, we may receive your name or username, email address, the content of your messages, and anything else you choose to share.

When you join through Patreon and connect Discord, those platforms share limited account information we need to grant and manage access — such as your username, user ID, membership tier, and membership status. Like most websites, our hosting provider and analytics may automatically collect technical data such as IP address, device and browser type, operating system, referring/exit pages, pages viewed, and timestamps, for security, performance, and analytics purposes.
